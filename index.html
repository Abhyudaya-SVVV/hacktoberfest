About Open Source

Open source projects allow anyone to view, modify, and enhance the codebase. By contributing, you can improve your skills and make meaningful contributions to real-world projects.

Why Contribute?

  • Learn how to work on large projects
  • Collaborate with developers around the world
  • Get feedback on your work
  • Enhance your portfolio and shine

What is Hacktoberfest?

Hacktoberfest is an annual event held in October that encourages people to contribute to open-source projects. Organized by DigitalOcean and GitHub, it invites developers of all skill levels to submit pull requests to public repositories. The goal is to promote collaboration, learning, and support for the open-source community.
